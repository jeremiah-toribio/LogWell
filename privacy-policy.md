# Privacy Policy

**LogWell**
*Last updated: May 15, 2026*

---

## Overview

LogWell is designed with your privacy in mind. All personal health and activity data you log — sleep, workouts, and focus sessions — is stored exclusively on your device and never collected, transmitted, or shared by us.

---

## Data You Log

All data you enter into LogWell (sleep logs, workout logs, focus logs, labels, ratings) is stored locally on your device using your device's internal storage. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us
- Is not shared with any third party
- Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

---

## Apple HealthKit

If you choose to connect Apple Health, LogWell may read and write health data (such as workout metrics) to and from the Apple Health app on your device. This data remains on your device and is governed by Apple's privacy practices. We do not transmit HealthKit data to any server.

---

## Advertising

LogWell displays ads provided by **Google AdMob**. To serve ads, Google may collect:

- Device identifiers (such as the Identifier for Advertisers / IDFA)
- General device information and usage data

Before showing ads, LogWell will ask for your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown and your IDFA will not be used for ad targeting.

For more information on how Google uses this data, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed through **RevenueCat** and **Apple**. RevenueCat receives anonymized purchase receipts to validate your subscription status. No personal information beyond what Apple provides during a standard App Store transaction is shared.

For more information, see [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

- Your name, email, or contact information
- Your location
- Your health or activity logs
- Any personally identifiable information

---

## Children's Privacy

LogWell is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
